Useful principles

               - "Not Invented Here" syndrome != A Good Thing
                 - No code sharing = No needback
                 - Treat code as «Continuously Improving Summary of a Public Conversation»

               - Remember your audience!
                 - DO write code that makes people give feedback on *what* you do
                 - DO NOT write code that makes people comment on *how* you do it
                 - Follow conventions & best practices
                 - The "Principle of Least Astonishment" is your friend
